Key Findings

1. Small Czech Sentence Embeddings (Seznam.cz) ✅ HIGH CONFIDENCE

Paper: Bednář et al., "Some Like It Small: Czech Sentence Embedding Models for Industry Applications" (IAAI 2024)
arXiv: https://arxiv.org/abs/2311.13921

What it is: - 14M parameter models (vs 110M Base models) = 8x smaller, 5x faster inference - Uses knowledge distillation + Czech-English pre-training + contrastive learning - Production-deployed at Seznam.cz (won IAAI Innovative Application Award)

Performance: - Small models: 87.60 on STS tasks - Base models: 82.91 on STS tasks - Competitive performance despite massive size reduction

Why it matters for dictpert: - Current: Seznam/retromae-small-cs already used - Opportunity: Validate performance on dialectal/historical text (not tested in paper) - Trade-off: Trained on modern standard Czech — may struggle with DIALEKT archaic forms

Action items: - [x] Paper downloaded - [ ] Test Seznam small models on dialectal entries from SNČJ/ČJA - [ ] Compare retrieval quality vs current retromae-small-cs - [ ] Measure inference latency improvement in production

2. Hybrid Neural+Dictionary Morphology (UDPipe 2 + MorfFlex) ✅ HIGH CONFIDENCE

Paper: Straková & Straka, "Open-Source Web Service with Morphological Dictionary-Supplemented Deep Learning for Morphosyntactic Analysis of Czech" (TSD 2024)
arXiv: https://arxiv.org/abs/2406.12422
Springer: https://link.springer.com/chapter/10.1007/978-3-031-70563-2_22

What it is: - Combines UDPipe 2 deep learning with MorfFlex dictionary rescoring at inference time - Dictionary constrains neural predictions to valid form-tag-edit-rule pairs

Performance: - Lemmatization: 99.15% accuracy (50% error reduction vs MorphoDiTa, 35% vs UDPipe 2 alone) - POS tagging: 98.44% accuracy (58% error reduction vs MorphoDiTa, 16% vs UDPipe 2) - OOV handling: 91.71% accuracy (dictionary preserves neural generalization)

Trade-off: - Speed: 60 words/sec vs MorphoDiTa's 10-200K words/sec - 1000–3000× slower — may not suit real-time RAG

Why it matters for dictpert: - Current: Majka (fast, ~98% accuracy) - Opportunity: Further 35% error reduction on lemmatization - Challenge: Speed penalty — need to batch or pre-lemmatize offline

Action items: - [x] Paper downloaded - [ ] Benchmark UDPipe 2 + MorfFlex on dialectal forms (inflected queries from eval) - [ ] Measure actual throughput impact in dictpert RAG loop - [ ] Explore GPU acceleration or sparse rescoring to close speed gap

3. Hybrid Retrieval + Reranking ⚠️ MEDIUM CONFIDENCE (preprint)

Paper: "Hybrid Retrieval Combining BM25 and Semantic Vector Search" (arXiv 2026)
arXiv: https://arxiv.org/html/2605.01664v1

What it is: - BM25-style lexical search + semantic vector search - Cohere reranking substantially reorders results

Performance: - Only 8.8% of chunks retained same rank after reranking - 3 of 25 final top chunks were originally outside initial top 10

Why it matters for dictpert: - Current: FTS (lexical) + vector search (semantic) already implemented - Missing: Reranking layer to fuse results - Opportunity: Cohere Rerank API or open-source Czech reranker

Caveats: - 2026 preprint, not yet peer-reviewed - Biomedical domain, not Czech NLP - Similar results confirmed in other 2024-2025 studies (MRR +44pp on MS MARCO)

Action items: - [x] Paper downloaded - [ ] Test Cohere Rerank on Czech dialectology queries (Q09, Q15, Q17) - [ ] Compare reranking vs current FTS+VSEARCH fusion - [ ] Explore training a Czech-specific reranker on dialectology data

4. Domain-Specific Pre-training ✅ HIGH CONFIDENCE

Paper: "Comparison of Czech Transformers on Text Classification Tasks" (2021)
arXiv: https://arxiv.org/pdf/2107.10042

What it is: - Pre-train Czech BERT on domain-specific corpora - FERNET-News (1B news tokens) vs FERNET-C5 (340M general tokens)

Performance: - News-trained models excel on news classification - General models provide broader coverage

Why it matters for dictpert: - Current: General-purpose Seznam embeddings - Opportunity: Pre-train on dialectology corpora (ČJA+SNČJ+SPJMS full text) - Challenge: Need substantial dialectal text (100M+ tokens)

Action items: - [ ] Estimate total text size across all 6 dictionaries - [ ] If ≥100M tokens, consider domain-specific pre-training - [ ] Evaluate cost/benefit vs fine-tuning Seznam small models

5. Cross-Lingual Transfer Learning ⚠️ MEDIUM CONFIDENCE

Papers: - Štefánik et al., "Multilingual Instruction Fine-Tuning" (BSNLP 2023) - Cotterell & Heigold, "Cross-Lingual Character-Level Neural Morphological Tagging" (EMNLP 2017)

What it is: - Multilingual instruction fine-tuning improves in-context learning for Czech vs English-only - Cross-lingual character representations improve morphological tagging up to 30% in extreme low-resource scenarios (100 training sentences)

Caveats: - 30% improvement is MAXIMUM in extreme cases (100 sentences) - Most languages show smaller gains at 1000 sentences - Single-task target-language training can sometimes outperform multilingual

Why it matters for dictpert: - Opportunity: Polish/Slovak dialects share features with Czech dialects - Limited applicability: dictpert has plenty of Czech data (not low-resource)

Action items: - [ ] Investigate if Slavic cross-lingual models help with rare dialectal forms - [ ] Low priority — we have 6 full dictionaries, not 100 training sentences

6. Dialectal & Historical Czech Datasets ✅ HIGH CONFIDENCE

Datasets:

DIALEKT Corpus

  • Size: 126,131 tokens
  • Period: 1960s-1980s rural dialectal Czech
  • Speakers: Born late 19th-early 20th century
  • Annotation: Two-tier (dialectological primary, orthographic secondary)
  • Source: https://arxiv.org/html/2407.04010, https://wiki.korpus.cz/doku.php/cnk:dialekt

Posel od Čerchova

  • Size: 432 pages, 100 article-level summaries
  • Period: 19th-century historical Czech journal
  • License: CC BY-NC-SA 4.0
  • Source: https://arxiv.org/html/2511.18848, https://corpora.kiv.zcu.cz/posel_od_cerchova/

Why it matters for dictpert: - DIALEKT: Directly comparable to ČJA/SNČJ temporal coverage - Opportunity: Validate embeddings/lemmatizers on DIALEKT to test generalization - Opportunity: Benchmark HyDE/retrieval on historical Czech with Posel od Čerchova
